2. What is Variables:-
Variables in Python are a core concept in Python programming. They allow you to save data that may be accessed and modified throughout your code. Imagine you have a box where you keep your favorite items, like a book or a toy. In Python, variables work just like that box, but instead of toys, you store data like numbers and text.
